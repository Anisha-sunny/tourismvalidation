Explore Alappuzhaa

Officially called Alappuzha, Alleppey is the picture-perfect place known for its beautiful backwaters and the houseboats offering overnight stays.
The coastline of Alleppey offers some of the best beaches in Kerala with water sports during the dry season.
Its palm-fringed inter-connect network of canal backwaters attracts a lot of tourists from all over the world.
Coupled with other beautiful places in Kerala, Alleppey is often considered for a honeymoon or for a great family vacation.
There are plenty of houseboats, homestays, and rejuvenating Ayurvedic resorts that make staying in Alleppey brilliant.
