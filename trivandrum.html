Explore Thiruvananthapuram

Trivandrum (or Thiruvananthapuram) is the capital of the southern state of Kerala.
The place is known for its idyllic beaches and rich culture because of which it draws tourists from all over the country.
Trivandrum is also one of the cleanliest cities in India.
